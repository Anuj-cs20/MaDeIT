About IIITDM

Indian Institute of Information Technology Design and Manufacturing Kancheepuram is an Institute of National Importance that focuses on educating young minds in design-oriented engineering, producing graduates who are skilled, ambitious, and conscionable, ready to confront challenges and initiate change in the industry.

The institute's vision is to become a premier center of excellence in design and manufacturing, while its mission is to be a world-class apex center for education, research, development, and training in design and manufacturing.

About MaDeIT

MaDeIT Innovation Foundation is a non-profit organization established by the IIITDM. It is supported by the Ministry of Human Resource Development and the Department of Science and Technology, Govt. of India. MaDeIT aims to promote design-driven startups, primarily in the manufacturing, healthcare, and transportation sectors.

As a design-driven Technology Business Incubator, MaDeIT nurtures hard-tech startups and encourages technology-based entrepreneurship. With an excellent incubation ecosystem, it supports startups from IIITDM's students, faculty, and the broader ecosystem to drive innovation and build a stronger economy.
